## About Me

> I'm **Exyone**, a C#/.NET developer from Shandong. In the daytime, I write code; at night, I read, write, and take photos.
{: .prompt-tip }

I work as a developer, spending most of my time in front of a computer. I enjoy solving problems through code, and I like exploring niche technologies that others might overlook.

This blog is a small space where I record my learning notes and thoughts. I write about the bugs I encounter, the solutions I find, and the insights I gain along the way. If these words can be of help to someone passing by, that would be wonderful.

Beyond code, I have a few simple hobbies. I like keeping a journal, putting pen to paper to record the small moments of life. I'm fond of vintage fountain pens — there's something calming about the scratch of a nib on paper. I also enjoy photography, capturing everyday scenes that catch my eye: a street corner, a patch of light, a passing stranger.
