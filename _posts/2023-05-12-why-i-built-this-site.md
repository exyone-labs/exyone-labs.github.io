Thirteen. That's how old I was when this idea first hit me — I wanted a place of my own on the internet.

I'm not a seasoned developer yet. Right now, I've only learned a bit of .NET — console apps, some desktop applications. Weekends are usually spent on learning to code, writing little programs, then getting stuck on bugs that take days to figure out. It's frustrating, honestly. But that feeling of building something yourself, even if it's just a humble "Hello World!" — there's nothing quite like it.

As you can see, I'm not a web developer. But these days, the internet takes up most of everyone's time. While searching for information and resources, I came across many personal blogs. To be honest, I wanted one too — a corner of the internet that's just mine. Everything I write here, every random thought I jot down, becomes a webpage searchable on the internet. That's kind of magical, isn't it?

If you've read this far, thanks for stopping by. Feel free to look around, leave a comment, or just say hi. This little corner is always open.

今年我13岁，突然有了个念头，想在互联网上有个属于自己的地方。

我暂时还不是一个成熟的开发者，只是略微学习了一点.NET——写写控制台程序，做点桌面应用。周末的时间，多半耗在学编程上——写点小程序，然后被几个Bug卡住，一卡就是好几天。这种受挫的感觉说实话不太好受。但那种亲手做出点什么的感觉，哪怕只是个非常低级的"Hello World!"，也特别有成就感。

如您所见，我不是一个Web开发者。但在当下，互联网占据了许多人的大部分时间。我在查询资料和搜集信息时看到了许多个人博客。诚然，我也想要一个。这是一个独属于我自己的互联网空间，我在这里记录的东西，哪怕只是胡言乱语，都成了互联网上能被检索到的网页。想想还挺神奇的，不是吗？

如果你读到了这里，谢谢你愿意停留。随便看看，留个评论，或者只是打个招呼都好。这个小角落，永远敞开。
